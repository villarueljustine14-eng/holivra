Training Tips & Guidance

  • Balance Your Routine

    Mix cardio and strength training 2-3 times per week for optimal results. Alternate between different muscle groups to allow proper recovery.

  • Progressive Overload

    Gradually increase intensity, weight, or duration each week to continue making improvements and avoid plateaus in your fitness journey.

  • Rest & Recovery

    Muscles grow during rest. Ensure 7-9 hours of quality sleep and include active recovery days in your weekly schedule for best results.

  • Fuel Your Workouts

    Consume a balanced meal with protein and carbs 1-2 hours before training, and refuel with protein within 30 minutes after your workout.
